Portfolio Entry #2 – Research Task

Chosen Task: Story Writing

This is a short story about an embarrassing moment that happened to me.

One morning, I was already late for school. I rushed out of the house and took the bus as fast as possible.

When I arrived at school, I noticed that people were looking at me and smiling. At first, I did not understand why.

A friend came up to me and told me that I was wearing two different shoes. One was black and the other one was white.

I felt very embarrassed, but after a moment we all started laughing. I stayed at school like that for the whole day.

In the end, it was not such a big problem, and it taught me to stay calm even in embarrassing situations.
