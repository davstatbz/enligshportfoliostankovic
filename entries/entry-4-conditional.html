Portfolio Entry #4 – Conditional Story

If I had to go to school every day, my life would feel very different.

I only have school once a week and work four days, which gives me a lot of practical experience.

If I worked less and stayed more at school, I would probably miss learning things directly at work.

Sometimes work can be stressful, but it helps me become more independent and responsible also for my private life.

Looking back, I am happy with this system, because it fits me well and prepares me for the future.
