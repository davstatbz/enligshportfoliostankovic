Portfolio Entry #6 – Best of 2025

Albums I already knew

From the list, I already knew these artists/albums (at least by name): Bad Bunny – DeBÍ TiRAR MáS FOToS, Playboi Carti – MUSIC, PinkPantheress – Fancy That, and FKA twigs – EUSEXUA.

Albums I listened to from the list

I listened to one song from these five albums:

  • Addison Rae – Addison
  • Bad Bunny – DeBÍ TiRAR MáS FOToS
  • PinkPantheress – Fancy That
  • FKA twigs – EUSEXUA
  • Blood Orange – Essex Honey

My favourite album

My favourite from the five was PinkPantheress – Fancy That. I liked it because it feels light, catchy, and easy to listen to. It’s the kind of music I can play while walking or on the train.

Albums missing from the list

For me, the list could also include more big mainstream rap/pop albums. Pitchfork has a special taste, but I think popular albums can still be very good and deserve a spot too.

Best things of 2025 (non-music)

  • Making good progress in my apprenticeship (3rd year, Informatiker Plattformentwicklung)
  • Getting better at problem solving in IT
  • More time with friends and better balance between school and work
