Albums I listened to from the list

My favourite album

My favourite from the five was Bad Bunny – DeBÍ TiRAR MáS FOToS. I liked it because it feels light, catchy, and easy to listen to. I love the vibe that this album gives me. It’s perfect for a warm summer night I personally think.
